About Me

My name is Yuvraj, and I am a master artist and web developer based in Jamui, Bihar. With a profound passion for both the visual arts and technology, I have spent the last honing my skills across various artistic mediums, including charcoal, oil, acrylic, watercolor, and pastels.

As a web dev, I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Python, C, and React.js. My dual expertise allows me to create immersive digital experiences that blend artistry and functionality.

Balancing creativity and technical prowess, I'm constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible in both fields. With a keen focus on user experience, seamless integration of technology, and artistic expression, my work resonates with both technical and creative audiences.
